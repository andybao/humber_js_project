We are hiring!

Andre’s restaurant always welcomes those who has the passion for cooking and preparing memorable dishes and has the capability of creating various dishes for menu of the restaurant. There are some requirements to apply in our restaurants. You should have at least two years of experience in cooking industry. Knowledge of HACCP and hygiene regulations is required. One must be legally entitled to work in Canada and has good communication skills. Our restaurant provides various opportunities for career and personal development. If you are interested please send your resume.

Part and full time Kitchen staff

Andre’s restaurant is looking for individuals who are passionate for cooking and has worked before in kitchen. As our restaurants has the speciality of multi culture so we want to hire experts for all locations. There are foods according to cultures so we are hiring chefs from all cultures. Presentation of food is an crucial art so along with cooking a chef should know how to present the dishes to customers. Attention to detail and efficiency is required. One should have the knowledge of HACCP and hygiene regulations is required. If you are interested please send us your resume.

Part and full time service staff

Andre’s restaurant is looking for mortals who has good communication skills and ethical customer dealing for various staff member positions like waiters and receptionists. The candidates should be professional in their job title as they have to deliver every little knowledge of restaurant’s facilities and menus to the customers. Jobs are available for both part and full time at all locations. If you are interested please send us your resume.
