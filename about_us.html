OUR STORY

Andre's restaurant is famous restaurant in Canada due to its culture-wise four locations in Canada. Our main motive is to provide best services and delicious and unique dishes to our customers than at any other restaurant they ever visit. We understand the need of people that they are still looking for the typical traditional food, and that's fine with us. Andre's restaurant is NOT willing to serve any heavy, oily, unhealthy food and it donot compromise with quality. We want you to join the growing trend that most of the communities has joined and you will try Andre as a unique alternative not just to other restaurants, but to all other healthy food options out there.

The menu is the heart and soul of the restaurant because it depends upon the location that is different menu for different location. The menu of Andre's restaurant fluctuates with the locations as at every location menu belongs to one culture’s food. All the dishes served at restaurants are freshly cooked or baked as tasty and healthy food keeps our customer and our restaurant growing. Our main mission is to pay respect to every culture, their efforts and traditions.
